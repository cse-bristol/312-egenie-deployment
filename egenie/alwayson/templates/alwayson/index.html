Always-On Electricity

On this screen, we show how the overnight energy use of the building changes during the week, comparing each night's usage to that of the previous night. While some devices may have to be turned on overnight (servers, security lights, etc), other devices may have been left powered by mistake. If you see that more energy was consumed last night, then you might want to look into whether something wasn't switched off that could have been.
